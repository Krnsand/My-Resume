My Groups And Societies

Jane GBG

Jane is a non-profit organization dedicated to amplifying, empowering, inspiring, and fostering a supportive community for women in creative industries. The organization started out of a desire to achieve a greater exchange among creative women in the region, find role models and synergies, strengthen our roles in the labor market and try to quench our constant thirst for inspiration and knowledge. Jane GBG

Tjejer Kodar

Tjejer Kodar was born out of a frustration that all our ideas and projects needed to be realized by someone else - a programmer. In our search for courses, networks and contexts that suited us, we didn't really find anything. It almost seemed like a competition to see who could make programming as complicated and difficult to access as possible. For us, programming is a creative tool for digital creation. It should be fun, simple and social. Tjejer Kodar

Django Girls

Django Girls is a non-profit organization and a community that empowers and helps women to organize free, one-day programming workshops by providing tools, resources and support. We are a volunteer run organization with hundreds of people contributing to bring more amazing women into the world of technology. Django Girls
